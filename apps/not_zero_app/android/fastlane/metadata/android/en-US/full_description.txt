Not Zero is a task management and well-being application designed to help you stay motivated and organized. By combining productivity tools with gamification elements, Not Zero encourages you to accomplish your daily goals and build lasting habits.
<br><br>
<h2><b>Key Features:</b></h2>
<br>
• <b>Comprehensive Task Management:</b> Organize your to-dos with tags, sublists, and specific categories like "Do it someday" for long-term goals.<br>
• <b>Habit Tracking:</b> Build and maintain positive habits with built-in tracking.<br>
• <b>Daily Check-ins:</b> Start or end your day with reflection and planning.<br>
• <b>Gamification:</b> Turn productivity into a rewarding experience.<br>
• <b>Insightful Statistics:</b> Track your progress with basic statistics.<br>
• <b>Your Data, Your Control:</b> Enjoy a privacy-focused experience with local storage and full data export/import capabilities.<br>
• <b>Personalization:</b> Choose between light and dark themes to suit your style.<br>
<br>
Not Zero is free, open-source, and constantly evolving to bring you more features for a balanced and productive life. Start your journey to "Not Zero" days today!
